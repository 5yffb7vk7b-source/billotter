How these templates work

Click a template and the Billotter generator opens with that trade's usual line items already in place — session fees for photographers, revision rounds for designers, kill-fee terms for writers. Put in your own rates and client details, hit Download PDF, done.

There's no account and no upload: the generator is a single page that runs entirely in your browser and saves your invoices to your own device. The templates are just head starts — every field stays editable.
